Nigerian Jollof

Nigerian Jollof Rice or Jellof rice is a rich and incredibly tasty west African one-pot Meal. It is a very versatile dish.

Edikaikong soup

Edikaikong soup is a delightful Delicacy from the South Southern region of Nigeria (specifically Akwa Ibom and Cross River States) which never disappoints.

Afang Soup

Afang Soup (Spinach and Okazi leaves) – slimy-textured soup loaded with chunks of protein and spices and seasonings. A healthy way of adding more greens to your table.

Isi ewu

Isi ewu, goat head in spicy sauce is a delicacy from Igboland and Akwa Ibom State.
